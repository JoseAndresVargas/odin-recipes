Description

This is a simple and easy recipe that tastes great to those that love tuna. It's an omelette (or multiple omelettes to be more accurate) with a single ingredient, tuna, where the mix of flavours gives it its unique taste. Ingredients

  • 3 Eggs
  • 1 medium can of tuna
  • Salt
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Break the eggs into a bowl where you can mix them
  2. Add salt to the eggs
  3. Whisk them until you have a foam like consistency
  4. In a medium temperature pan (with oil), carefully add two tablespoons of the eggs into it
  5. Add tuna on top of the eggs that you placed on the pan
  6. Cover the top with one tablespoon of eggs (add another if it's not covered yet)
  7. After 3-5 minutes, carefully turn it around
  8. After 1-3 minutes, remove it from the pan, and there you have your first tuna omelette
  9. Repeat the process until you have no more eggs in the bowl
