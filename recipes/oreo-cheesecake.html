Oreo Cheesecake

Home

Description

This is a no oven oreo cheesecake recipe that I found a while ago on youtube. If you want the video version, you can watch it on the 매일맛나 delicious day channel.

Ingredients

  • 20 Oreo Cookies
  • 200g cream cheese
  • 200g whipped cream
  • 3 tablespoons of sugar
  • 3 tablespoons of melted butter

Steps

  1. Make 10 cookies into powder
  2. Add 3 tablespoons of melted butter to the cookie powder and mix
  3. Add cream cheese, whipped cream, and 5 cookies
  4. Add 3 spoons of sugar and mix
  5. Put cooking foil on the prepared square tube
  6. Add cookie powder and press flat
  7. Add the mixed cream cheese and make it flat
  8. Freeze for more than 4 hours into the freezer
  9. After 4 hours, cut into a good size to eat
  10. Decorate with 5 cookies
