Momofuku korean breakfast

Home

Description

This recipe is a variation of a traditional korean breakfast that I found via youtube. It has more ingredients than the original but it's still as simple and delicious as that one.

Ingredients

  • Sesame oil
  • Soy sauce
  • Two large eggs
  • Cooked white rice
  • Momofuku chili crunch
  • Turkey breast ham
  • Green onion

Steps

  1. Cut green onion and ham into little squares
  2. Add sesame oil to a pan and wait until it's hot enough (set the pan to medium heat)
  3. Add two eggs without breaking their yolk
  4. Carefully separate the egg white from the yolk and cook it
  5. When the egg white is cooked, add a tablespoon of sesame oil on the eggs
  6. Add a table spoon of soy sauce over the eggs
  7. Break the egg yolks and mix everything together
  8. Add the ham and mix (You can turn off the heat by this moment)
  9. When cooked (be careful not to dry the eggs), add it to a bowl with rice
  10. Add the green onion you sliced on top of the eggs
  11. Add a teaspoon of momofuku and mix together
